Framework Purpose

Use this framework to assess your organization's current cloud maturity, identify gaps, and create a roadmap for advancing through the maturity phases. Most organizations start at Tactical and progress toward Transformational capabilities.

Speaker Notes - Google Cloud Adoption Framework

Framework Overview:

  • Google Cloud's framework helps evaluate organizational maturity in adopting cloud technologies
  • Serves as both assessment tool and roadmap for cloud transformation
  • Based on real-world observations from thousands of cloud adoptions

Four Assessment Themes:

1. Learn (Graduation Cap):

  • Assesses quality and scale of learning programs
  • Tactical: Self-taught, third-party reliance
  • Strategic: Organized training, third-party assisted
  • Transformational: Peer learning and sharing, minimal external dependency

2. Lead (Users):

  • Extent of IT department support with leadership mandate
  • Tactical: Teams by function, heroic project manager
  • Strategic: New cross-functional cloud team
  • Transformational: Cross-functional feature teams, centralized platform team

3. Scale (Expand Arrows):

  • Usage of cloud-optimized services and operational automation
  • Tactical: Change is slow and risky, ops heavy
  • Strategic: Templates ensure good governance without manual toil
  • Transformational: All change is consistent, low risk, and quickly fixed

4. Secure (Shield):

  • Capability to protect environment from unauthorized access
  • Tactical: Fear of public internet, trust in private network
  • Strategic: Central identity, hybrid network
  • Transformational: Trust only the right people, devices, and services (Zero Trust)

Three Maturity Phases:

Tactical Phase:

  • No coherent plans covering all workloads
  • Interested in quick return on investments
  • Little disruption to IT organization

Strategic Phase:

  • Plan in place to develop individual workloads
  • Eye toward future scaling needs
  • Mid-term goal to streamline operations

Transformational Phase:

  • Cloud operations work smoothly
  • Use data from operations to improve IT business
  • Long-term goal of making IT engine of innovation

Using the Framework:

  • Assess current state across all four themes
  • Identify gaps and areas for improvement
  • Create roadmap for advancing maturity
  • Most organizations progress through phases over 2-3 years
  • Different themes may be at different maturity levels
